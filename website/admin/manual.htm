Product Information

Overview - The Rich Template allows everyday web users without any type of programming knowledge to update and maintain their web site.  Built with the client in mind, Rich Template enables your business to manage a dynamic website without paying the high fees for web masters or database administrators.  By utilizing easy to use administration screens, your web site content can be modified, organized, secured, published, distributed, archived, or removed by authorized personnel of your company.  In addition, the Rich Template offers many features and controls that you can set to make sure your content is displayed in a manner that suits your company needs.  The Rich Template can also be customized to implement special features or functionality that your company needs to fulfill particular business processes.  The infrastructure of the Rich Template is scalable to allow for easy add on functionality at any time.  Beyond the robust functionality of the Rich Template lies a professional web page design for your company.  All web designs are unique and custom.  Our designers pay particular attention to your company image, and corporate branding.  The Rich Template is different from other content management systems because it uses a browser based program that can be accessed anywhere with a computer and an Internet connection, it is scalable to fit your company needs, comes wrapped in a professional web design, and is more affordable than other solutions.

 

 

Using Your Rich Template Administration Area (Backend)

2.1  Logging In to your Rich Template - Your Rich Template is a secure site that can only be accessed by authorized personnel.  User access will be predetermined, and authorized administrators will receive a username and password to input into a login screen much like the one below.  Once a user is logged in, they will be redirected to the Rich Template homepage where they can begin to manage Rich Template content.

*** If your Rich Template has a security flow installed, an authorized user can add new users and passwords to give other personnel access to the Rich Template.

2.1  Navigating Your Backend - Your Rich Template backend is essentially an additional web site.  Links and sections are created according to the content that can be managed.  Rich Template pages can be easily navigated to by a left hand side navigation area that links to the different sections of your backend.

  • As you can see, your Rich Template backend uses a familiar navigation structure that will allow you to locate specific content sections.  Simply click on the links to navigate to the appropriate section you are intending to manage.
  • Your left hand navigation will be consistent and can be located on every page of your Rich Template backend.  You will quickly become familiar with your navigation and will be able to get to any section of your Rich Template backend with one quick click to the appropriate page.
  • In addition, your Rich Template navigation will always have a link back to your website that will enable you to quickly view your changes.  If your Rich Template has a preview capability (a feature that allows you to view changes that have not yet been published) a link to view content that has not yet been published is also available.
  • A link to support@homepagefx.com can give you immediate feedback about your Rich Template, and a link to Homepagefx.com will also be always present on your Rich Template backend.

 

2.2  Add, Edit, and Delete Content - Once you get to a particular section of your Rich Template that you intend to manage, you are presented with a screen much like the one below.  This management screen allows you to perform the majority of your needs.  As you can see there are a number of elements to this screen. 

  • Date - Gives you the date of the particular content
  • Title - This field could be a number of things, but mostly it gives you a reference to the content you will be updating.
  • Edit - An EDIT button is present, and links you to a page that allows you to edit existing content.
  • Delete - The DELETE button will delete existing content ( a security alert box pops up when you press delete to make sure you are certain this content should be deleted.
  • Archive - By pressing the ARCHIVE button you set your content to an "inactive" state that will no longer be visible on your website.  However, you can easily re-instate this content by pressing the RESTORE button.
  • Add - At the bottom of this screen you will see an ADD button.  By pressing the add button you will be redirected to a web page that allows you to add new web site content to this particular section.

2.21 Adding Content to Your Rich Template

  • At the administrative screen you have an a ADD button at the bottom of the page.

  • By pressing the ADD button, you are redirected to a web page with particular empty form fields.

  • Add your content within these fields, paying attention to formatting issues such as dates.

  • Simply press the ADD button at the bottom of the form, and your new content will be updated in real time.

  • Pages that require particular formatting will use a custom built WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) editor that allows you to make formatting changes to your text.  Below is a sample WYSIWYG editor.  However the WYSIWYG editor can only be used with Internet Explorer 5.0 or higher.

2.22 Editing Content on Your Rich Template

  • When you press the EDIT button from the administration screen, you are redirected to a form much like the one you see above.  This form allows you to change any of the content within the particular form fields.

  • Once you have made your edits, press the EDIT button on the form, and your changes will be made and can be viewed on your website.

2.23 Deleting Content on Your Rich Template

  • When you press the DELETE button from the administration screen, you are immediately alerted with an ALERT BOX  that asks you if you are sure you wish to delete this item.

  • If you are sure, then press OK and that item will be deleted automatically.

  • If you are uncertain that you want to delete this item permanently, than we suggest you archive this item by pressing the ARCHIVE button.

2.24 Archiving Content on Your Rich Template

  • By pressing the ARCHIVE button from the administration screen, you are essentially "inactivating" that particular item.  Although this item will be removed from the web site, it will not be removed from the database.

  • When an item is ARCHIVED, the ARCHIVE button is replaced with a RESTORE button.  To re-activate the item (get it back on to your website), simply press the RESTORE button.

  • ***You will only see the ARCHIVE mode if your Rich Template has the preview functionality installed.
